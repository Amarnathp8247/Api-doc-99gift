GET /product/infos/{id}

Get Individual Product Details

Retrieve detailed information about a specific product by its ID. Token is not required but can be passed in the header for authenticated requests.

Note: If the denomination array is empty, it means the product is out of stock.

Request Parameters

Attribute Type Required Description Sample
id integer Yes The unique identifier of the product 694
